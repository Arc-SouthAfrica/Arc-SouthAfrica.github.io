Meet Nathma Samie – Elected 2023 ARC SA Ambassador

Committed to bringing about greater psychological and environmental well-being through active engagement with communities and agencies for change.

About

Educator and researcher with a degree in Psychology and HIV/AIDS Counselling with certification in Climate Change Mitigation in Developing Nations. Currently is engaged in post graduate studies that explores the interplay between biopsychosocial well-being and climate change. Nathma strongly believes that education and timely access to resources are vital to grassroot empowerment of communities, so they are able to shape their own sustainable and equitable futures.

She was elected as a Climate Change Champion in 2022, as part of the Climate Ambition to Accountability Project. Completed the Cradle Moon bootcamp hosted by World Wildlife Fund (WWF)- South Africa (SA) , Climate Action Network (CAN)-SA, (IEJ) Institute for Economic Justice– SA, and the Presidential Climate Commission (PCC)- SA, which was co-funded by the European Union. Thereafter she was elected as team leader for proposition paper creation for government consideration focused on increased meaningful engagement of youth.
