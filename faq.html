Frequently Asked Questions.

How can I get involved with ARC?

We appreciate your interest in ARC! To get involved, you can explore our website and participate in our educational programs, workshops, and events. You can also join our community and connect with like-minded individuals, organizations, and experts in the field of environmental sustainability.

How can I download and use the printable educational resources?

To download and use the printable educational resources, simply navigate to the respective resource page on our website. Look for the download link or button associated with the resource, and click on it to initiate the download. Once downloaded, you can print and use the resource in classrooms or at home to engage young learners in discussions about our planet and its future.

Does ARC offer any certifications

Yes, we provide certificates of completion for learners who successfully complete certain educational programs or courses offered by ARC. These certificates serve as a recognition of your participation and accomplishment in specific programs. The eligibility criteria for obtaining a certificate may vary depending on the program.
