Welcome to the Education hub of ARC

Your ultimate resource for promoting environmental literacy and empowering learners to understand and take action on climate change and environmental conservation.

We provide a range of educational materials, including interactive stories, informative articles, and engaging activities, to enhance literacy and foster a deeper connection with our planet.

In this section, we are delighted to present an engaging and informative story that aims to educate children and teachers about the significance of protecting our environment. Join us on a captivating journey as we explore the wonders of nature alongside Mhambi, Langa, and Cebisa, the wise honeybee.

Through this enchanting tale, we will venture into the breathtaking Cape Floral Kingdom, home to a myriad of magical plants and captivating landscapes. Along the way, we will learn valuable lessons about climate change, environmental conservation, and the importance of preserving our natural resources.

To facilitate interactive learning experiences, we offer a printable version of this educational resource. Feel free to download it here and use it in classrooms or at home to engage young learners in meaningful discussions about our planet and its future.

Stay tuned for updates and new resources, as we continue to expand our offerings to support the learning journey of individuals of all ages.
