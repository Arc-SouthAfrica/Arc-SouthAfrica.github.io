About African Resources Counsel (ARC)

Mission

Provide transformational education that empowers environmental consciousness initiatives.

Vision

A sustainable world shaped by climate conscious education.

African Resources Counsel (ARC) serves communities in Africa by providing non-formal education inspired by art therapeutic techniques and embedded with environmental conscious practices. The main objective is to empower children and young adults with tools for self-expression and lifelong learning needed to build their own sustainable future.

ARC is a grassroots initiative that coordinates and collaborates locally and internationally to present training courses, workshops, festivals, and exhibitions that will be enriching to educators and students. Special focus is placed on creative arts, drama, theatre, music, photography, and videography, that have environmental conservation, child and human rights, diversity and inclusion, and democracy at the heart of them.

Every project embarked upon has to meet the ARC criteria to receive the green light:

  • A = Awareness and Accessibility: The project must increase awareness about social issues and increase youth accessibility to resources to bring about change.
  • R = Resilience and Reliability: Projects should encourage mental resilience and connections to reliable networks and stakeholders.
  • C = Conscious Commitment: Projects should reflect renewed commitments to environmental consciousness in communities.
